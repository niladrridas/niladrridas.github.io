About Me

I am a proud member of the 2024 graduating class at the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Mandi, specializing in Data Science and Machine Learning. Currently, I am undertaking an internship at CloudNet India, a renowned academic and training institute in Kolkata. In my role as a Hardware and Network Engineer, I am gaining extensive hands-on experience with industry-standard software tools, including VMware Workstation, Red Hat Enterprise Linux, Ubuntu, Windows 10, Windows Server 2019, Cisco Packet Tracer, and GNS3. This internship allows me to continuously enhance my skills and knowledge, enabling me to effectively support and optimize our systems and infrastructure. Additionally, during my time at Lovely Professional University, I developed microcontroller programs at the assembly level using software IDEs such as Keil Micro Vision and engaged in VLSI System Design with VHDL in Xilinx Vivado 2022.
